And I love watching movies. This website is where I share my curated lists of anime movies I've watched alongside my thoughts and recommendations. Whether you're seeking your next favorite movie or exploring the genre, this is a space to connect and celebrate our love for anime movies.

Favorite Anime Movies

Your Name.

I’ll never forget how this movie made me feel. It’s a beautiful mix of emotions—love, longing, and this overwhelming feeling of connection. The idea of two people swapping lives and finding each other across time? That’s pure magic. I’d recommend it to anyone who believes in soulmates or destiny.

Trailer
Weathering with You

This movie beautifully intertwines themes of love, sacrifice, and nature’s power with stunning visuals. The soundtrack is mesmerizing and complements the heartfelt moments. For those who enjoyed Your Name or want a compelling story about the choices we make for love, this is a must-watch.

Trailer
The Garden of Words

It's like a poem come to life. It's so quiet and intimate, and I loved how the rain almost seemed to be its character. It made me think of how some relationships seem to come at the right moment in our lives, even though they won't last forever. I recommend it for a cozy, rainy day when you want to feel something beautiful.

Trailer
5 Centimeters per Second

This film is one way to show how time and distance can affect a relationship and how memories remain even as life moves on. It is very masterly how Makoto Shinkai could show the quiet ache of love that was never fulfilled and how time passes. The train scenes and the falling cherry blossoms are metaphors that go deep.

Trailer
Suzume

It's like an emotional roller coaster when watching Suzume. Loss, healing, and unexpected connections are involved. Additionally, there were moments of warmth and laughter mixed with grief that made me realize that even though doors close, new ones will open. It is something that I'd recommend to anyone who loves fantasy and heartfelt storytelling.

Trailer
A Silent Voice

It is one of those movies that broke me in the best way ever. It is natural and honest when it comes to redemption, learning how to forgive not just people but to learn about forgiveness oneself. I cry with this movie and feel it, especially in a way that makes you know how hard it is to get into the person. It is a film that touches your heart, leaving you to think.

Trailer
I Want to Eat Your Pancreas

This emotionally powerful film beautifully explores the fleeting nature of life and the profound impact of human connection. The genuine relationship between the shy protagonist and the lively Sakura is heartwarming and inspiring, serving as a reminder to completely enjoy life's short moments. This film will leave you cherishing the little moments and reflecting on the impact others have on your journey.

Trailer
Hotarubi no Mori e

This film, set in a mystical forest, beautifully portrays an unattainable love between a human and a spirit. The peaceful visuals and pure bond between the characters are both heartwarming and sorrowful due to their inevitable separation. However, if you, like me, struggle with certain characters (like the child), it may leave you feeling a mix of anger and sadness.

Trailer
Howl's Moving Castle

This movie is pure magic. I love how it combines a whimsical fantasy world with a heartfelt love story. Plus, Howl is such a dreamy character—it’s impossible not to fall for him. If you’re looking for something like stepping into a fairytale, you can’t go wrong with this one.
